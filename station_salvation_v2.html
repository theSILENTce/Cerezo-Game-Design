Station Salvation v2

Overview

Station Salvation v2 is map/level refinement of it's predecessor, Station Salvation , a third-person sci-fi shooter project that I helped develop in my Game Design 2 course during my second semester at CSU East Bay. This map/level design differs from its predecessor, by the inclusion of more playable areas as well as added environmental details.

Progress So Far

Since this personal project is currently under development, I cannot share too much information at the moment. But what I can share is a singular screenshot of one of the completed sections of the map, which is the player start area.
