Animarium

Overview

Animarium is a fictional floating island from two popular children shows from the early 2000s, Hyakujuu Sentai Gaoranger and its American adaptation Power Rangers Wild Force. This 3D model of the turtle shapped floating island, is a digital asset that can be loaded into any project.

Concept/General Idea

I wanted to create one of the major assets from the already existing I.P. (Intellectual Property) of Toei's Hyakujuu Sentai Gaoranger and Sabanâ€™s Power Rangers Wild Force, by creating a 3D model rendering of the iconic floating island, Animarium.

Process

I first started this creation process by looking at reference images from the show. After observing the reference image, I began sketching the image to help me better understand the scale and dimensions of the island.

After the sketching process, I then went into Blender to craft a 3D model of the island.

While creating a 3D rendering from scratch, I decided to try a different approach to bringing the island to life. Within Blender, I opened up a new project, placed the original reference image of the Animarium from the show, and then placed it into Blender's shading process. It was from there that I was able to manipulate the flat-shaded model and shape it into a 3D model.
