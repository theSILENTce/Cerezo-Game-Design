Picnic (2022)

Overview

Picnic is an augmented reality 3D still life piece consisting of potluck snacks from my childhood. The snacks represented in the piece are Filipino sweat rice cake (biko), a watermelon slice, and a capri sun pouch, all anchored on top of a 5x5 image of a flower. Each item was created in Blender, textured in Adobe Dimensions, and grouped together in Adobe Aero.

Process

Just like any other project, everything begins with a sketch. For this specific project I wanted to create a digital still life that would tap into a person's nostalgia, while also telling a story of my own personal childhood.

After coming up with the concept behind my sketch, I then went into blender to create 3D models of the sketched items. Then went into Adobe's Blender to add textures.

The final step was then to set the time of day, that would best suit the environment and give the players/viewers a feel of curiosity.
