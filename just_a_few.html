Just A Few (2021)

Overview

Just A Few is a augmented reality gallery experince, showcasing a few of the projects I had created during my first two semesters at CSU East Bay.

Process

My first step for this project was to sketch out an inital layout of how I wanteed the area to be sapced, and how I would want the view to progress through the gallery.

After I had sketched the layout, I then began to collect the projects that I had recently completed during the time, as well as collect and create various assets to help better the user experince to my virtual gallery.

Once I had gathered all the assets that I had needed, I then began to place them with in the Adobe Aero application and began to script interations within the space to add to the immersion of the digital space.
